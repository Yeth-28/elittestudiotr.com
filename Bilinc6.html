NEWS | Bilinc6 | Gameplay

Bilinc6 is a fan-made game developed in approximately two weeks. As such, it's completely normal to encounter various bugs or issues. This project is not directly related to our usual area of work; it was created upon special request and primarily designed to generate video and social media content. We've enhanced the experience to be more engaging and dynamic for that purpose.
To make it accessible to everyone, we’ve released the game for free on our itch.io page. You're welcome to try it out and share your thoughts or feedback with us!

NEWS | Bilinc6 | Posts

One of the additional and entertaining features within Bilinc6 is the environmental effects and the sudden occurrence of 12 different types of effects or mechanical changes. These introduce a unique gameplay experience where your world can be turned upside down at any moment, keys might behave differently, your speed could suddenly increase, or your health might unexpectedly decrease. Since these events are unpredictable, you must be prepared for anything and fight against the toxic elements with caution.

About the Game

This is not your traditional game. This is a fan-powered entertainment experience. A short but chaotic dive into the subconscious, full of weird moments and tongue-in-cheek humor. It’s not for everyone — and it doesn’t try to be.

"Bilinç Altı" is a short-form fan game, made in a limited timeframe with a focus on creativity, not perfection. As such, occasional bugs or quirks are normal and part of the experience. The game doesn’t aim to be flawless — it aims to be fun, expressive, and a love letter to the fan community.

Objective

Your mission is clear: Rescue the streamer (Elraenn). But the path is anything but ordinary. Navigate strange obstacles, survive odd encounters, and decode the subconscious madness.

Gameplay Progression

Chapter 1: Find scattered note papers in the house and unlock the door Chapter 2: Survive for 3 minutes while being chased by toxic creatures Chapter 3: Overcome a challenging obstacle course Chapter 4: Discover the streamer’s location and finish the mission
