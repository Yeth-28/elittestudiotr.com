NEWS | Our Journey Through the GMTK Game Jam 2025

We joined GMTK Game Jam 2025 — one of the world’s biggest game jams with over 36,000 teams. With just 96 hours, things got intense fast. Our design team started strong, but we struggled early with development. We weren’t aiming for awards — we just wanted to finish. And we did. Despite hurdles, we leaned into the fun, worked as a team, and delivered. Was it perfect? No. Was it ours? 100%. GMTK wasn’t just about making a game — it was about staying creative under pressure, growing together, and proving we could finish what we started. And yeah we shipped it.

About the Game

This is not your traditional game. This is a fan-powered entertainment experience. A short but chaotic dive into the subconscious, full of weird moments and tongue-in-cheek humor. It’s not for everyone — and it doesn’t try to be.

"Bilinç Altı" is a short-form fan game, made in a limited timeframe with a focus on creativity, not perfection. As such, occasional bugs or quirks are normal and part of the experience. The game doesn’t aim to be flawless — it aims to be fun, expressive, and a love letter to the fan community.

Objective

Your mission is clear: Rescue the streamer (Elraenn). But the path is anything but ordinary. Navigate strange obstacles, survive odd encounters, and decode the subconscious madness.

Gameplay Progression

Chapter 1: Find scattered note papers in the house and unlock the door Chapter 2: Survive for 3 minutes while being chased by toxic creatures Chapter 3: Overcome a challenging obstacle course Chapter 4: Discover the streamer’s location and finish the mission
